The Regular Show

Regular Show is an American animatwed sitcom created by J.G Quintel for Cartoon Netowrk.It ran from September 6, 2010, to January, 2017, over the course eight seasons and 261 episodes. The series folows the lives of two 23-year old friends, Mordecai and Rigby.

Mordecai Michael Peterson

He is one of the two protagonists of the 2010 Cartoon Network animated series Regular Show, and Regular Show: The Movie. He is a 23-year-old blue jay and is Rigby and Benson's best friend. He works as a groundskeeper at the park but often like to slack off with Rigby.

Roderick "Rigby" Terrence Riggerson

He is one of the protagonists of Regular Show. He is a 23-year-old anthropomorphic bown raccoon who works as a groundkeeper at The Park. He has been best friends with Mordecai sijnce childhood and is more prone to slacking off than Mordecai is.

Benson Tyler Dunwoody

One of the main characters of Regular Show. He is a gumball machine, and was originally as The Park's manager. He has a short temper and tends to yell at his employees when they are not working particularly Mordecai and Rigby.

Skips Miller

He is the tritagonist of Regular Show. He is an immortal yeti who works at the park as a groundkeeper. He helps Benson a lot, and fixes many of the mistakes that Mordecai and Rigby make
